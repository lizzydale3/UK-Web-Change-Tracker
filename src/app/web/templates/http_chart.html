About this metric
Source: Cloudflare Radar HTTP Requests timeseries (normalized index). Values are per-location medians scaled to 1.0.
Normalization: Daily cyclical patterns (business hours, night traffic) are algorithmically removed to highlight meaningful deviations from baseline.
Use: Deviations capture macro traffic shifts (outages, throttling, holidays). The dotted 24h MA shows trend direction.
Look for: Sustained dips below ~0.8, sudden step changes, or divergence from seasonal patterns around the event window.
