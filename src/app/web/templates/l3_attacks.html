Understanding L3 Attacks
Target (bytes): Volume of attack traffic that was blocked/mitigated when targeting UK-based servers, networks, or infrastructure. This shows how much malicious traffic the country is defending against.
Origin (bytes): Volume of attack traffic that originated from UK-based networks/ASNs targeting other countries. This shows if the UK is being used as an attack launchpad.
Why separate: Different attack directions have different security implications. High target values indicate the country is under attack; high origin values suggest compromised infrastructure.
